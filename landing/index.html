Our Story

Notes Monkey was born from a simple frustration: the inability to find important notes when you need them most. Our founders, a team of engineers and designers from India's top tech companies, experienced this pain daily.

We believed that note-taking shouldn't be complicated. It should be as natural as having a conversation, as organized as your thoughts, and as secure as your most precious memories. That's why we built Notes Monkey with Indian users in mind - understanding our unique workflow patterns, collaboration styles, and security needs.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of users across India, from students preparing for competitive exams to teams building the next generation of Indian startups. Our mission remains simple: to make your ideas accessible, organized, and secure.
